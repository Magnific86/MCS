HANDYCOAT

Handycoat EZ-Skim

Handycoat EZ-Skim is a ready-to-use, high quality, thin layer putty for finishing most interior surfaces and preparing them for any type of finish. Due to the selected composition of binders, fillers and special additives, it is characterized by increased plasticity and ease of application.

Handycoat All-Purpose

Handycoat All-Purpose is a ready-to-use, high-quality, all-purpose putty for leveling and preparing most interior surfaces for any type of finish, as well as for repairing subfloor irregularities and filling joints in drywall partitions.

Handycoat Interior

Handycoat Interior is a ready-to-use putty designed for application on most types of building surfaces, allowing to obtain a high quality surface for painting. Handcoat Interior is intended for repair and alignment of both old and preparation of new interior surfaces. Handcoat Interior - used for leveling and creating a high-quality surface for painting, for wallpapering and applying decorative coatings, and also for repairing minor damage to plastered and painted surfaces. Handcoat Interior can be applied to previously puttied and painted surfaces, tongue-and-groove plates, fiberglass, bases made of sheet materials (GKL, Aquapanel, etc.), cement-containing and gypsum plasters, concrete surfaces, as well as applied on problematic grounds having “hairy” cracks Handycoat Interior is not suitable for application to surfaces painted with oil paints or chalk-based paints, varnishes, enamels, not intended for application to plastic and metal surfaces.
